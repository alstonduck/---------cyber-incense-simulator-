# ⛩️ 電子上香模擬器 (Cyber Incense Simulator) 🎰

> **「量子登仙，賽博飛升。生前功德無量，死後冥鈔萬貫。」**  
> A dark-comedy cyber-spiritual casino crawler that blends traditional East Asian temple worship, electronic woodblock tapping, and ancestral ritual offerings with hardcore cyberpunk-themed wagering mechanics.

---

## 🎮 Core Gameplay Loop & Philosophy

In the year 2026, the spiritual plane has fully migrated to high-frequency quantum cloud servers. Deities, ghost-bureaucrats, and deceased ancestors no longer accept traditional smoke offerings and paper sacrifices. Instead, they demand **Cyber Merit (功德)** and **Digital Hell Money (冥紙)** wagers.

Players traverse the neon-lit **Cyber Temple (賽博大雄寶殿)**, cultivating resources and betting against celestial forces across 17 distinct, deeply integrated ritual mini-games to purge their dynamic **Karma (業障)** and ultimately challenge the Jade Emperor for absolute spiritual ascendance.

```
       [ Tap Woodblock ] ──> Generates Base Merit
              │
              ▼
   [ Purchase Cyber Incense ] ──> Reduces Karma & Buffs Ritual Win Chances
              │
              ▼
     [ Play 16 Rituals ] ──> Gamble Merit / Hell Money for massive returns
              │
              ▼
    [ The 17th Finale ] ──> Stake ALL wealth on Jade Emperor's Divine Judgment
```

## 💰 Resource Mechanics

The game environment runs on three interdependent spiritual metrics:

1. **Merit Points (功德 - Gongde)**: Your high-tier spiritual currency. Grown by playing skill-adjacent rituals, hitting jackpots, or tapping the electronic wooden fish. Required to make your bid for nirvana in the final tier.
2. **Hell Money (冥紙 - Mingzhi)**: Your transactional Underworld currency. Used to purchase custom cyber incense formulas, fuel slot machines, and participate in low-level luck games.
3. **Karma (業障 - Yezhang)**: A dynamic risk metric representing accumulated spiritual debt. High Karma directly dampens your luck, reducing your success rates in games. Karma escalates when you lose wagers but is purged by burning incense or hitting special purges.

## 💨 The Cyber Incense Burners

Before starting any ritual, players can ignite up to **3 simultaneous electronic incense sticks** inside the primary temple dashboard. Each incense stick burns in real-time, tick-by-tick, venting spiritual smoke to passively decay Karma and grant active luck multipliers:

*   **Sandalwood (檀香 - Standard Quality)**: Low cost, slow passive Karma relief.
*   **Agarwood (沉香 - High Quality)**: Moderate cost, active speed-reducing Karma decay.
*   **Ambergris (龍涎香 - Divine Quality)**: Premium cost, reduces Karma rapidly and slightly boosts game resolution luck.
*   **Dragon's Saliva (龍涎聖香 - Forbidden Quantum Formula)**: High cost, ultimate luck stabilizer. Reduces Karma extremely fast and offsets high-karma penalty scaling.

## 🏮 The 17 Divine Rituals

The rituals are categorized into distinct gaming types based on player agency, mathematical design, and risk profile:

### Category A: Skill-Adjacent Rituals (Player Decisions Matter)

1. **求籤廿一 (Fortune Stick 21 / Blackjack)**:
   * **Lore**: Shake the copper cylinder to pull bamboo fortune sticks under the watchful eyes of the Fortune God.
   * **Mechanic**: Classic 21-point rules. Players draw sticks with randomized values representing cosmic fortunes. Go over 21, and the "God's Wrath" (Bust) instantly disintegrates the wager.
2. **神魔對決 (Deities vs. Demons / Baccarat)**:
   * **Lore**: Back the golden host of Heaven or the dark swarms of the Underworld.
   * **Mechanic**: Classic Baccarat card sums modulo 10 aiming for 9. Supports Tie (平局) bets yielding an 8x spiritual return.
3. **冥府梭哈 (Underworld Hold 'Em / Poker)**:
   * **Lore**: Deal five talismanic cards marked with elements (Metal, Wood, Water, Fire, Earth).
   * **Mechanic**: Select which talismanic locks to hold, then redraw to construct high-tier elemental alignments (Pairs, Trios, Flushes, or the Ultimate Cosmic Harmony).
4. **街頭擲筊 (Divination Blocks / Street Craps)**:
   * **Lore**: Toss the crescent wooden crescent blocks (筊杯) onto the temple floor.
   * **Mechanic**: Roll outcome determines reward. *Holy Answer* (聖筊 - one flat, one round) builds your multiplier streak. *Angry Answer* (哭筊 - two round) breaks your streak and burns your stake.

### Category B: Exploitable Rituals (Patience, Logic, & Memory)

5. **十八地獄塔 (Pagoda of Hell / Dragon Tower)**:
   * **Lore**: Descend level-by-level through the 18 layers of cold Buddhist hells.
   * **Mechanic**: Each level contains three sealed urns. Two contain Holy Lotuses (success, raises the multiplier), while one hides the Evil Eye (instantly wipes all accumulated loot). Cash out at any level, or gamble to climb further.
6. **清明掃墓 (Tomb Sweeper / Minesweeper)**:
   * **Lore**: Walk through the ancestral graveyard to weed the plots.
   * **Mechanic**: Minesweeper grid where safe plots reveal ancestral smiles (+Multiplier) and bad plots trigger a restless, wailing spirit that resets your earnings.

### Category C: Hybrid Rituals (Reflexes, Timing, & Predictive Models)

7. **焚燒冥紙 (Burning Joss Paper / Crash)**:
   * **Lore**: Throw high stacks of paper currency into the temple's central automated quantum furnace.
   * **Mechanic**: The multipliers rise exponentially as the furnace heats. Players must click "CASH OUT" before a random cosmic gust of wind crashes the flame, burning all assets to ash.
8. **六道輪迴 (Reincarnation Wheel / Roulette)**:
   * **Lore**: Spin the wheel representing the Buddhist Wheel of Life.
   * **Mechanic**: Bet on the Human, Heaven, Asura, Animal, Hungry Ghost, or Hell realms. Low-density realms (Hungry Ghost / Hell) carry much higher payout multipliers (up to 15x).
9. **功德箱彈珠 (Donation Box Drop / Plinko)**:
   * **Lore**: Drop brass coins into the peg-laden automated donations slot.
   * **Mechanic**: The coin bounces physically through a geometric layout of pins, falling into randomized multiplier columns at the bottom.
10. **星宿連線 (Constellation Keno / Keno)**:
    * **Lore**: Align coordinates on the Celestial Star Map to unlock planetary energy.
    * **Mechanic**: Pick 1 to 10 star nodes. The game fires 20 stellar light beams. The more matched nodes, the higher the cosmic multiplier.
11. **橫渡奈何橋 (Crossing the River Styx / Frogger-style Crossing)**:
    * **Lore**: Guide a fragile paper servant across the raging currents of the Styx.
    * **Mechanic**: Advance lane-by-lane. Each safe step builds a cashout value. Swept away by Underworld logs or whirlpools, you lose all progression.

### Category D: Pure Luck Rituals (Unbiased Cosmic Randomness)

12. **妙法蓮華輪 (Mandala Wheel / Wheel of Fortune)**:
    * **Lore**: Spin the grand rotating lotus prayer mandala.
    * **Mechanic**: Instant-win or instant-loss sector selections with zero player control. Includes cosmic tax audits and jackpot modifiers.
13. **佛光普照 (Aura Wheel / Money Wheel)**:
    * **Lore**: Predict which sacred neon light aura will wash over the altar next.
    * **Mechanic**: High-contrast, vibrant color selections (Jade Green, Imperial Gold, Void Purple, Blood Red).
14. **貢品老虎機 (Fruit Offering Slots / Slot Machine)**:
    * **Lore**: Pull the handle on a 3-reel classic slot machine laden with spiritual offerings.
    * **Mechanic**: Match Peaches of Immortality, Roast Pigs, Golden Urns, and Red Envelopes. Landing 3 Golden Urns triggers the grand temple jackpot!
15. **紙扎駿馬奔騰 (Paper Horse Race / Horse Race)**:
    * **Lore**: Sponsor one of four legendary paper horses racing along an ash-strewn virtual racecourse.
    * **Mechanic**: Real-time racing simulator with updating coordinate positions and random speed boosts.
16. **陰陽羅盤 (Yin-Yang Dial / Hi-Lo)**:
    * **Lore**: Predict the oscillation of the compass's needle.
    * **Mechanic**: Guess if the next generated random value will be Higher (Yang) or Lower (Yin) than the previous reference index.

### Category E: The Final Confrontation

17. **終極天命擲筊 (The Finale / Ultimate Divination)**:
    * **Lore**: Face the Supreme Emperor of Heaven on your 17th stage.
    * **Mechanic**: Stake your ENTIRE accumulated net worth (all Merit and Hell Money) on a single, final throw of the Sacred Divination Blocks.
    * **Ascendance**: Yielding a *Holy Answer (聖筊)* permanently ascends your soul to Nirvana, granting victory.
    * **Damnation**: Yielding a *Angry Answer (哭筊)* condemns your soul to the deepest pits of the abyss, initiating a hard wipe of state.

## 🔊 Premium Audio Synthesis Engine

To preserve high performance, eliminate heavy asset load overhead, and ensure immediate responsiveness, the application utilizes a highly custom, native **Web Audio API Sound Engine** (`/src/utils/sound.ts`). 

### Redesigned Woodblock Sound (木魚)
The original clicking sound was replaced with an **organic, ultra-soft, mellow, and hollow temple woodblock sound**. It is specifically crafted using three parallel, envelope-shaped audio pathways to ensure *zero harsh high-frequency transients*:
1.  **Velvet Mallet Strike Transient**: A low-frequency triangle oscillator pulse (2.2x to 1.1x fundamental frequency) ramping down quickly in 12ms with a soft 1ms attack to completely eliminate clipping pops.
2.  **Mellow Hollow Chamber**: A pure sine wave oscillating at 210Hz with a warm 90ms exponential decay, simulating the resonance of physical hollowed cherry-wood chambers.
3.  **Wood Shell Resonance**: A secondary subtle sine wave at 1.58x the fundamental frequency decaying rapidly in 60ms to add the physical texture of a wooden shell.
4.  **Lowpass Filter Isolation**: All outputs are piped through a lowpass filter set strictly to 500Hz with a Q factor of 1.8, sealing any residual harshness out of the sound.

### Apple Watch-Style Tactile UI Click Feedback
To enhance all interactive interfaces (tabs, betting selectors, and navigation triggers), a **whisper-quiet, incredibly satisfying, ultra-elegant UI Click** has been integrated:
*   Uses a single, highly refined sine wave starting at 360Hz and dropping exponentially to 150Hz over 20ms.
*   Runs through a strict lowpass filter at 400Hz to strip out click-pops, and applies a microscopic 1ms linear attack.
*   Gives the distinct physical impression of premium electronic glass haptic engine clicks.

### Environmental Effects
*   **Temple Gong**: Rich, complex fm-synthesis style bell gong with high detune and wide frequency spread.
*   **Ascendance Chime**: Sparkling multi-frequency celestial arpeggio.
*   **Abyssal Hum**: Low-frequency resonant filter sweep triggered upon failure or wrath states.
